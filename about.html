All About Annie

Hi! I'm Annie - a chicken-owning, contemporary-dancing, dance-educating, strategic-communicating, PR-freelancing, soon-to-be-web-developing Seattleite.

I was born and raised in West Virginia - refer to John Denver's "Country Roads" for more about WV.
After receiving degrees in both Corporate Communications and Dance from Elon University in North Carolina, I moved around a lot.
I spent a summer dancing in NYC, traveled to New Zealand on a work visa, moved to Charleston SC for a freelance producing gig and then found my way to Seattle.
5+ years later, I'm loving it and ready for a career upgrade!

I am a regular student at Velocity Dance Center, as well as the Development Committee Chair on Velocity's Board of Directors.

I love Velocity so much that I volunteer as the emcee/auctioneer for their annual fundraiser and auction. We raised $80k at the 2014 event. Boom.

I work for the awesome woman pictured above, KT Niehoff.
She is a Seattle-based choreographer and entrepreneur.
After years of being the Producing Manager for her company, I am now the Brand Director of her new artist residency + event space 10 degrees.

I've spent the past 3+ years teaching creative dance and modern technique to kids and teens. Aren't they adorable?!

I've learned my lesson about surprise phone hacks after years of teaching teens.

My fiance Blake is my favorite person on earth. He's a pastry chef turned GM at Macrina Bakery, and we're getting married in May 2015.

Thanks for reading!
